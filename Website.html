What?

Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated all over Maharashtra as the coming of the God of Knowledge, Lord Ganesh. Many people believe that when one starts a new venture, Lord Ganesh must be prayed for sucess in the same.

How is it celebrated? People get idols of Ganpati in their houses and worship it. At the end of some days, the idol is immersed into water. This season is a seson full of joy and love.
